# Important Updates and Corrections Made After the Guidebook Was Published

## Companion notice for 2026 Utah Bear / Cougar Guidebook

### Corrections

**Page 73:**
In the fall limited-entry bear hunt table, the Monroe unit (BR7237) was included in error and has been removed from the guidebook. Due to the 2025 fire, last year's permit was extended to 2026 and it would be a biological impact to offer an additional permit this year.

The digital edition of this guidebook has been updated with this information. (Feb. 12, 2026)
